Our journey

Fifty-Four Years of Legacy

1970
Founded by Dr. Natarajan, a visionary physician who established Madhava Medicals as a trusted neighbourhood pharmacy in Chittoor — committed to honest, affordable medicine for every family.
1985
Under Dr. Natarajan's stewardship, the clinic expanded its medicine inventory and deepened roots in the Chittoor community. His son, Dr. Jaghannathan, began training alongside him, learning the values of patient-first care.
1990
Dr. Jaghannathan took over Madhava Medicals from his father Dr. Natarajan, carrying forward a 20-year legacy with renewed energy. He introduced formal medical consultations, expanded the pharmacy, and strengthened the clinic's reputation across Chittoor.
2000
Celebrated serving 5,000 patients. Expanded the medicine inventory to over 300 products including branded and quality generic options. AYUSH registration achieved.
2010
Stock expanded to 500+ medicines. A second generation of care continued under Dr. Jaghannathan, with plans to bring the next generation into the practice — keeping the family tradition alive.
2024
Serving over 12,000 patients and growing. Launched digital appointment booking and WhatsApp consultations — the founding values of Dr. Natarajan, carried forward with modern care by Dr. Jaghannathan.

Our story

Five Decades of Trust

Founded in 1970 by Dr. Natarajan, Madhava Medicals Clinic began as a small neighbourhood pharmacy in Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh — built on a single belief: every family deserves honest, affordable healthcare.

In 1990, Dr. Natarajan handed the clinic to his son, Dr. Jaghannathan, who carried that legacy forward with dedication and expanded the clinic into a full-service medical destination. Today, over five decades on, that same spirit of compassionate, family-rooted care continues to serve the community of Chittoor.
